RECIPE CUPCAKE

INGREDIENTS

PASTA:

  • 3 gems
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla essence
  • 250 ml of milk
  • 1 cup of oil
  • 3 egg whites
  • 3 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of yeast Filling and topping to taste, here we use dulce de leche

PREPARATION MODE

  1. Beat the yolk, sugar and vanilla in a mixer.
  2. Then add the milk and oil and beat some more.
  3. Add the snow whites, the wheat flour and the yeast and beat more with the help of a fouet (do not beat in the mixer so that the yeast can act).
  4. Pour the batter into cupcake tins (don't fill too much) and bake until golden.
  5. Once baked and cooled, poke a hole in the center of each cupcake with a knife.
  6. Reserve the cap.
  7. Place the chosen filling in a pastry bag and place it in the hole in the center of the cupcake.
  8. Put the lid on and cover the cupcake with the desired frosting, here we use dulce de leche to fill and to cover.
  9. Add your favorite candies.
