RECIPE MAC AND CHEESE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 package of cooked pasta
  • 1 tablespoon of butter salt to taste
  • 1 box of sour cream
  • 1 cup (tea) milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (grated)
  • 1 cup grated provolone cheese
  • 1 cup(s) grated Parmesan cheese

PREPARATION MODE

  1. In a pan, melt the butter, add the cream and milk.
  2. Heat, but do not let it boil.
  3. Add the cheeses, add salt to taste.
  4. Mix the macaroni into this mixture.
  5. Serve it hot.
